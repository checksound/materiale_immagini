[Compressor Head](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLOU2XLYxmsIJGErt5rrCqaSGTMyyqNt2H)  

Compressor Head is a 6 episode video series explaining the theory and practice of compression algorithms. This is information that is rarely, if ever covered in Computer Science curriculum. This series is the brainchild of Developer Advocate Colt McAnlis and produced by Google Scalable Outreach team.
